Finding antimatter tracks tutorial

Hi! Thank you for helping science by identifying tracks from antiparticles.

You will be shown an animation as seen bellow and be asked to mark the particle tracks

You will be able to pause the animation and go through it frame by frame.

When you see a track starting, pause the animation and click on its origin in the first frame you can see it.

Now you can mark the end point of the track in the last frame you see it with the same method.

A line will be drawn between the two points.

You can add as many tracks as you are able to identify. Then press "Add Tracks" to save your progress and move on to the next animation.

Oh, we almost forgot. The tracks you have marked will be used to build a 3D model of the particles trajectories which can be seen on the front page.
