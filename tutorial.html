Finding antimatter tracks tutorial

Hi! Thank you for helping science by identifying tracks from antiparticles.

You will be shown an animation as seen below and be asked to mark the particle tracks in the emulsion.

You will be able to pause the animation and increase or decrease its speed.

When you see a track appearing, try to click on its origin in the first frame you can see it (the "Pause", "Increase speed" and "Decrease speed" buttons may come in handy for that).

Now you can follow the same procedure to mark the end of the track when you see it dissapear.

A line will be drawn between the two points.

You can add as many tracks as you are able to identify. Then press "Save drawn tracks" to save your progress and move on to the next animation.

Oh, we almost forgot. The tracks you have marked will be used to build a 3D model of the particles trajectories which can be seen on the front page.

Enjoy your tracking!
