Flickr Person Finder tutorial

Hi! This is a demo application that shows how you can do pattern recognition on pictures or images using the PyBossa framework in Crowdcrafting.org.

The application is really simple. It loads a photo from Flickr and asks you this question: Do you see a human in this photo?

You will have 3 possible answers:

  • Yes,
  • No, and
  • I don't know

All you have to do is to click in one of the three possible answers and you will be done. This demo application could be adapted for more complex pattern recognition problems.
